# CSAT in Hiver

CSAT (Customer Satisfaction) measures satisfaction via surveys sent after resolution.

## Why CSAT Might Not Appear

- CSAT feature disabled at `Settings > Analytics > CSAT`.
- CSAT not included in your current plan tier.
- Surveys not being triggered due to resolution status not set.
- Insufficient permissions to view CSAT dashboards.
- Data processing delay; wait 15â€“30 minutes.

## Enabling CSAT

1. Navigate to `Settings > Analytics > CSAT`.
2. Enable CSAT surveys and configure timing.
3. Customize survey question and rating scale.
4. Ensure agents resolve conversations to trigger surveys.

## Troubleshooting Steps

- Confirm plan tier includes CSAT.
- Check permissions for your role.
- Verify resolution status change is applied.
- Inspect event logs for survey trigger failures.
- Contact support if surveys fail consistently.
